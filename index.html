Research

I have been working and learning by assiting a principal investigator regarding their research on the reconstruction of images based on fMRI data. We have been using the NSD dataset. My work has been focused on recreating, verifying, and analyzing results from previous and similar projects. Recently we have begun to focus on using latent diffusion models to recreate subjects fMRI scans. Throughout this experience, I have acquired knowledge about the foundational principles of neural networks, delving into essential concepts such as activation functions, weight optimization, backpropagation, and the architecture of convolutional and tansformer neural networks.
